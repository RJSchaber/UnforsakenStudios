Chris Sica

Hello, my name is Christopher and I'm the Lead Gameplay Designer for Unforsaken Studios! A few things you should know about me is that I'm a big fan of music and I can play the guitar, bass, ukulele, and keyboard. I grew up in South Florida and went to a university in Orlando. Now, I live in Colorado where I enjoy hiking, bike riding, yoga, soccer, hockey, and spending time with my dog, Nymeria. To top off how awesome I am, I am a history nerd who loves to read books!

I prefer games that follow a good storyline or are heavily focused in strategy. Some of my favorites are Civilization, Spyro, Age of Empires, RollerCoaster Tycoon, Bioshock, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, and Uncharted. I've always been interested in educational games that are used for teaching. Soon I'll be working on releasing dev (PREFER TO USE FULL WORD FOR "DEV") blog tutorials to help teach people about how Unforsaken Studios sets up their prototypes. I've always loved the video game industry and wanted to be a game designer. So far, I've worked on a wide range of games from Madden NFL,Tiger Woods PGA Tour, Life is Strange, as well as other various mobile games as a freelance contractor. I can't wait for you to experience the amazing designs me and my team at Unforsaken Studios are working on!
