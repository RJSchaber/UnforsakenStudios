Kent Lindsay

This is Kent. You might recognize him from our Instagram page @unforsakenstudios or his personal page @kentflight22. He was born in Carbondale, IL and grew up on a farm near a tiny town called Alto Pass in southern Illinois. Growing up Kent spent a lot of time in nature, playing basketball and TCGs like Magic, Star Trek and Lord of the Rings. He also loved computers and computer games starting out with Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego, Oregon Trail and Jeopardy. As he grew up so did his taste in games playing the original Deus Ex and RTS titles like Warcraft II and Command and Conquer.

After high school Kent studied Business and Computer Science at North Central University in Minneapolis where he roomed with Ray Johannessen. During this time they formed a lasting friendship. Kent always wanted to start up his own company building and repairing computers, so he joined Office Depot as a Technology Specialist and quickly rose to become an Assistant Store Manager. He is with Unforsaken Studios because he wanted to join his long time friends Ray and Daniel Noel in creating something unique and meaningful. He uses his experience in the retail environment and in management to help keep everything moving along while providing an unique perspective. He spends a majority of his time managing the social media for the company and working on misc. projects including his own TCG.
